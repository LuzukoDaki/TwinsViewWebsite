About Us

Founded by passionate sport enthusiast, Mandis Pepetha, Zukile Daki, and Luzuko Daki, Sport in Twinsview is focused on growing the sport media and sport culture in South Africa. Sport in Twinsview is dedicated to producing quality sport videos
